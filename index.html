About Sarah

I loved journalism before I could properly comprehend the term and am working to build a future doing what I love.

I graduated from the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications and the School of Information Studies at Syracuse University in May 2023 with a dual degree in newspaper and online journalism and information management and technology with a concentration in data analytics.

Following graduation, I moved from New York to Chattanooga, Tennesssee, where I worked as a digital producer and journalist at the Chattanooga Times Free Press.

I am currently the politics reporter at the Chattanooga Times Free Press, covering the local and statewide politics beat.

For any news-related inquiries, please reach out to sdolgin@timesfreepress.com or fill out my tip form on the contact page.
